# The Flash Theme - Color Scheme

**Theme Applied:** ✅ Red and Yellow/Gold Color Scheme

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Red (Crimson):** `#DC143C` - Main action color, buttons, accents
- **Dark Red:** `#C41E3A` - Secondary red for gradients
- **Gold/Yellow:** `#FFD700` - Highlights, active states, borders
- **Dark Gold:** `#FFA500` - Secondary gold for gradients

### Background
- **Gradient:** Red to Gold (`#DC143C` → `#C41E3A` → `#FFD700`)
- **Cards:** White with gold borders and red shadows
- **Glass Effect:** Semi-transparent with backdrop blur

---

## Design Elements

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Red gradient with gold border and text
- **Hover:** Enhanced shadow and slight lift effect
- **Active:** Gold glow effect

### Cards & Containers
- **Background:** White with subtle gold tint
- **Border:** Gold (`#FFD700`) 2px solid
- **Shadow:** Red glow effect

### Text
- **Headings:** Red to gold gradient
- **Active States:** Gold with glow effect
- **Links:** Red with gold hover

### Status Indicators
- **Active:** Gold with glow
- **Success:** Gold background with red accents
- **Error:** Red background with gold accents

## Visual Effects

### Glows & Shadows
- Gold text shadows for emphasis
- Red button shadows for depth
- Combined red and gold glows on active elements

### Animations
- Pulse effect on active workflow steps
- Smooth transitions on hover
- Transform effects on buttons
